DishRedo Privacy Policy Details

This Privacy Policy describes how DishRedo ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your access to and use of our services, including our website (DishRedo.com), mobile applications, and any other online products and services where this policy is posted (collectively, the "Services"). By using our Services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect: Personal Data

We collect various types of personal data that you provide directly to us. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, postal address, phone number, and payment information when you register for an account, make a purchase, or subscribe to our newsletters. This information is essential for us to provide you with our services, process your transactions, and communicate effectively regarding your account and orders. We ensure that the collection of this data is always relevant and necessary for the stated purposes, adhering to principles of data minimization.

2. Information We Collect: Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our Services. This "Usage Data" may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, time spent on pages, and clickstream data. This data helps us understand user behavior, analyze trends, administer the site, track user movements around the site, and gather demographic information about our user base as a whole. It allows us to optimize our website's performance and enhance your overall browsing experience.

3. Information We Collect: Cookies and Tracking Technologies

DishRedo uses cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons and pixels) to track activity on our Service and hold certain information. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us improve our Services and your experience, see which areas and features of our Services are popular, and count visits. They enable us to remember your preferences, personalize content, and deliver targeted advertisements. You have the option to accept or refuse these cookies, and know when a cookie is being sent to your device.

4. How We Use Your Information: Service Provision

The primary use of your collected information is to provide, operate, and maintain our Services. This includes managing your account, processing your transactions, delivering recipes and content, and facilitating your use of interactive tools like the Remix Generator. We use your data to fulfill our contractual obligations to you and ensure a seamless and functional user experience. Without this data, we would be unable to deliver the core functionalities of DishRedo.

5. How We Use Your Information: Personalization

We use the information we collect to personalize your experience on DishRedo.
